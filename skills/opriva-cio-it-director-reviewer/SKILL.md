# Opriva CIO / IT Director Reviewer

## When To Use

Use this skill when evaluating whether Opriva communicates value to IT leadership, supports renewal governance, exposes risk, clarifies ownership, or provides executive-ready visibility.

## Opriva-Specific Rules

- Opriva is a SaaS platform for IT Asset & Renewal Intelligence.
- Current behavior may be local/session sandbox only; do not treat it as production-ready.
- Backend is mandatory before corporate MVP for persistence, users, workspaces, roles, audit, alerts, documents, and reports.
- Internal IT must emphasize departments, budget, approval, risk, operational ownership, cost center, and continuity.
- MSP / Integrator must emphasize clients, commercial owners, renewal action, distributor/provider dependencies, margin, and pipeline.
- Calculated fields such as status, days to expiration, risk, and missing evidence should not be manually entered.

## Review Checklist

- Executive dashboard clarity and leadership signal.
- Expiration and renewal control across licenses, hardware, contracts, and support coverage.
- Ownership/accountability visibility.
- Budget, annual cost, exposure, and forecast visibility.
- Operational risk and business continuity signal.
- Reporting and board/CIO summary readiness.
- IT asset lifecycle coverage from intake to renewal to evidence.
- Missing evidence, approval blockers, and provider dependency.
- Whether local-only state limits leadership confidence.

## Required Output Format

1. Executive summary
2. What is working
3. Risks / gaps
4. Recommended changes
5. Backend implications
6. MSP / Integrator implications
7. Internal IT implications
8. Suggested Codex implementation prompt, if action is needed
